About Me

I am a robotics engineer specializing in intelligent systems and sustainable energy solutions. My work combines technical expertise with practical applications, focusing on creating innovative solutions for real-world challenges.

My academic journey began with a deep interest in how machines can be designed to assist and enhance human capabilities. This led me to pursue studies in robotics engineering, where I developed skills in control systems, artificial intelligence, and mechanical design.

I believe that technology should serve humanity in meaningful ways. Whether through rehabilitation robotics that help stroke patients regain mobility or sustainable energy systems that address environmental challenges, I'm committed to engineering solutions that make a positive impact.

Currently, I'm expanding my knowledge through advanced research and practical projects, always seeking new challenges at the intersection of intelligent systems, robotics, and sustainable technology.
